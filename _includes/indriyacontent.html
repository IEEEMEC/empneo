INDRIYA - THE SCIENCE FAIR




INDRIYA invites abstracts from schools all over the state. The best 20 abstracts will be select- ed and the teams will be intimated via the respective school authorities. These teams shall be assigned mentors to guide and support them if needed. INDRIYA is a team event and the maximum number of partici- pants allowed in a team is 5. Empneo 2.0 regis- tration is not compulsory to participate in the Science Fair and quiz.

Rules

  1. The project ideas should be presentable as a still or working model.
  2. Students studying in classes 9-12 (as of the academic year 2018-2019) are eligible to participate.
  3. Participation can be on an individual level, or as a team, each consisting a maximum of 4 members.
  4. Each team should submit an abstract in not more than 600 words.
  5. The abstract should be submitted at the time of registration.
  6. Based on the quality of the abstract, teams will be shortlisted, and mentors will be assigned to help the participants improve or build their project, lab facilities will also be provided to students in colleges around the student's locality who are associated with our event.
  7. The final project will be put up for exhibition at MEC on July 13th,where a panel of judges will decide the winners.
  8. The last date for registration is 20th June 2018.
