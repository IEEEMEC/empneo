ALCHEMY- QUIZ



Alchemy is a general quiz for school students of same school or otherwise, in the science-tech flavour which comprise two sections: The Prelims and The Finals. The top six teams qualify into the finals, which will have four rounds in the regular Bounce and Pounce approach. Winners receive prizes worth 8K. The quiz aims to increase the interest of students in science and technology right from the school level, and provide a fun filled oppor- tunity for the same.
