ALCHEMY- QUIZ




Alchemy is a general quiz for school students of same school or otherwise, in the science-tech flavour which comprise two sections: The Prelims and The Finals. The top six teams qualify into the finals, which will have four rounds in the regular Bounce and Pounce approach. Winners receive prizes worth 8K. The quiz aims to increase the interest of students in science and technology right from the school level, and provide a fun filled opportunity for the same.



  1. Prelims will be written.
  2. Top 6 teams will be selected for the final round from the prelims.
  3. A team can consist of two members.
  4. Cross School teams allowed.
  5. Final round will consist of 4 rounds based on Pounce and Bounce style.
  6. Students studying in classes 9-12 (as of the academic year 2018-2019) are eligible to participate.
